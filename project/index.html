Welcome to the World of Personal Color Analysis!

Ever wonder why certain colors make you feel confident, while others leave you looking washed out? Personal Color Analysis can help you unlock the magic of color and bring out your best features. It’s all about finding the shades that make you glow and complement your unique undertones.

WHY DOES IT MATTER?

Feel your best: Wearing the right colors can boost your mood and confidence.

Save time: A color palette that works for you means shopping gets easier. No more wasting money on clothes that don’t quite look right!

Sustainable style: When you buy things that match your palette, you’re more likely to wear them often, making your wardrobe more versatile and sustainable.

SO, WHAT’S YOUR COLOR PALETTE?

Personal Color Analysis used to be based on just four seasons — Winter, Spring, Summer, and Autumn — but it’s now more accurate and personalized with twelve seasonal palettes.

Each palette is defined by a unique mix of how light or dark, warm or cool, and bright or muted your natural coloring is.

Here’s a sneak peek at the 12 palettes:

  • Winter: True Winter, Bright Winter, Dark Winter
  • Spring: True Spring, Bright Spring, Light Spring
  • Summer: True Summer, Light Summer, Soft Summer
  • Autumn: True Autumn, Dark Autumn, Soft Autumn
